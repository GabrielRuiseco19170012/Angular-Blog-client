About Us.

We are a UTT youth company who dream of being software developers, this website is our first web application that we developed during this semester, we try to imitate facebook, we replicate its most basic functions, such as making publications and commenting on the publications of other users, obviously registering users, we develop the server part and the client part, all team members work in back-end and front-end.
